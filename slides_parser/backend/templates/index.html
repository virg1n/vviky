How does it work?

  • Complete sign up

    To create presentations, you need to go through a simple registration. You can sign up by click on this text

  • Imagine & Write prompt

    After registration, you will be redirected to the page with the creation of a presentation, where you can choose the number of slides and choose any topic that does not violate the rules of use of vviky. Now click on the "create" button

  • Wait & Download

    Now, you need to wait from one to three minutes so that we can create your presentation. Immediately after creation, you will see a button on the screen to download the presentation
